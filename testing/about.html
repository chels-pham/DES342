About


When Prometheus stole fire from heaven, Zeus, the king of the gods,
took vengeance by presenting Pandora to Prometheus' brother Epimetheus.
Pandora opened a jar left in her care containing sickness, death and many
other unspecified evils which were then released into the world. Even though
Pandora had released pain and suffering upon the world, she had also allowed
Hope to follow them.
